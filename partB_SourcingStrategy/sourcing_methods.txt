Sourcing Methods
To find Federer-profile companies across India, I would use:

Government & Regulatory Databases

MCA (Ministry of Corporate Affairs) → check filings for revenue bands, leadership succession.

DSIR-recognized R&D centres → signals IP-based differentiation.

USFDA/EU-GMP approval lists → identify specialty pharma/biotech producers.

PLI scheme beneficiaries → companies in growth sectors with government tailwinds.

Industry Associations & Directories

Pharmexcil, CII, FICCI, ASSOCHAM → membership lists of specialty producers.

Genome Valley directory (Hyderabad), Biotech Park directories (Pune, Bengaluru) → concentrated clusters.

Trade Shows & Expos

BioAsia (Hyderabad), India Pharma Expo, ChemExpo India, Medical Device Expo → exhibitors are typically differentiated producers.

LinkedIn & Job Portals

Search for companies with ERP/SAP hiring, R&D roles, plant expansion jobs → signals systems maturity and growth.

Export Data

DGFT export-import database → specialty exporters in chemicals, biotech, MedTech.

Limitations: Some data is outdated, not all companies disclose revenue, and smaller specialty producers may not appear in mainstream directories. Manual verification is always required.
