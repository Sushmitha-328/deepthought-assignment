# 1000-Company Scale-Up Proposal

Objective: Build a list of 1000 ICP-qualified companies within one month.


## Plan

### Universe Building
- Collect raw lists from **MCA filings, DSIR-recognized R&D centres, Pharmexcil, Genome Valley directories, trade expos**.
- Scrape exhibitor directories and government databases.
- Expected universe: ~3000 companies.

### Automated Filtering
- Apply **E1/E2 gates** using scraping + AI prompts:
  - E1 → check for “manufacturing facility,” “plant,” “R&D centre.”
  - E2 → confirm India operational presence.
- Narrow down to ~1500 companies.

### Federer Scoring Automation
- Use AI + scripts to tag evidence for **C3–C8**:
  - Patents → Google Patents, DSIR.
  - Decision-maker pedigree → LinkedIn.
  - Growth signals → hiring data, press releases.
  - Systems maturity → ERP/SAP mentions in job postings.
- Expected yield: ~1000 companies.

### Quality Control
- Manual review of borderline cases (score 40–59).
- Remove PE-owned, subsidiaries, traders.
- Finalize **1000 ICP-qualified companies**.
- Deliver CSV + methodology + fail list.


## Realistic Yield
- Start: 3000 raw → Filter: 1500 → Score: 1000 → Final: 1000 ICP-qualified.
- Yield rate ~33%.



## Tools & Sources
- **Government**: MCA, DSIR, USFDA/EU-GMP lists, PLI scheme beneficiaries.
- **Industry**: Pharmexcil, CII, FICCI, trade expos (BioAsia, ChemExpo).
- **Digital**: LinkedIn hiring data, company websites, AI scraping scripts.
